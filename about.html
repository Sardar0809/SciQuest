About SciQuest

Built by a Pakistani science student, for Pakistani science students

Our Mission

Making academic support accessible to all

SciQuest was born from a simple frustration: science students in Pakistan have incredible knowledge and skills, but no structured way to help each other or earn from that expertise. A third-year Parasitology student knows more about helminth identification than most — yet there's no platform for them to offer that knowledge to a junior student struggling with a thesis chapter.

We built SciQuest to change that. A zero-fee, peer-to-peer academic task exchange where students post what they need, helpers earn fairly, and the entire science community grows stronger together.

No middlemen. No fees. No barriers. Just students helping students.

Our Values

What we stand for

🆓

Always Free

We will never charge students for using SciQuest. Every feature is free for every student, from registration to task completion.

🔒

Safe & Verified

Only verified university students can join. Admin oversight ensures quality and removes bad actors quickly.

🌍

Inclusive

We support Urdu and regional languages. Rural, urban, every university, every field — SciQuest is for all Pakistani science students.

🏆

Merit-Based

The leaderboard rewards effort and excellence. Good helpers rise naturally. Quality work builds lasting reputation.

🤝

Community First

This is a community, not a marketplace. We want students to build relationships, learn from each other, and grow together.

🔬

Science-Focused

Built specifically for biology and science students — our fields, categories, and features are tailored to scientific academic work.
