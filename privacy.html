Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Summary: We collect only what we need. We never sell your data. Your WhatsApp/contact details are only shared with your task partner. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for SciQuest, we collect:

  • Full name and university email address
  • University name, degree program, and field of study
  • Optional: Profile photo, WhatsApp number, short bio
  • Skills you add to your profile
  • Tasks you post or accept and their details
  • Ratings and reviews you give or receive

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile to other verified SciQuest users
  • To match you with relevant tasks in your field
  • To display your ranking on the weekly leaderboard
  • To send you relevant platform notifications (you can opt out)
  • To review and moderate content to keep the platform safe
  • To contact you regarding your account if needed

3. Information Sharing

Your WhatsApp number is only visible to your task partner after a task is accepted. It is never shown publicly. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, advertiser, or organization — ever.

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely. Passwords are encrypted. We use HTTPS on all pages. Admin access to user data is strictly limited to moderation purposes only.

5. Your Rights

  • You can update or delete your profile information at any time
  • You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data
  • You can opt out of notification emails at any time
  • You can request an export of your data by emailing us

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels of any kind.
